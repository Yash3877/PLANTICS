Neem

Scientific Name : Azadirechta indica juss
Family : Meliaceae
Common Name :: Neem (कडूलबिं)
Azadirechta indica is native to the northeast of the Indian subcontinent and to Indochina. It is a fast growing tree that can reach a height of 15–20 metres. It is evergreen, shedding many of its leaves during the dry winter months. The fruit is a smooth, olive like drupe which varies in shape from elongate oval to nearly roundish.
Ecological Value:Nimba tree serves as the natural air purifier.
Cultural Value:Nimba leaves are used at the time of Gudhi padwa
Economical Value:It has innumerable uses, to mention a few. Products made from Nimba trees have been used in the traditional medicine of India for centuries, for treating skin troubles. Nimba is a key ingredient in natural pesticides. Nimba cake is used as a fertilizer.
