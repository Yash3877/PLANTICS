Cycus

Scientific Name : Cycus species.
Family : Cycloceae
Common Name :: Cycus (सायकस)
This is small to medium sized tree with trunk covered by scaly leaves. Foliage leaves are pinniately compound ,arranged spirally on trunk. Leaflets are articulated with prominent midrib. Scaly leaves are persistent, brown in colour. Plants are dioecious bears male and female cones as reproductive structures. These cones produces gamets to produce naked seed as product of fertilization. These plants are used ornamental plants. Some species are used as vegetable. Starch also obtained from this plant.
