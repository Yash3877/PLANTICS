Hibiscus

Scientific Name : Hibiscus
Family : Malvaceae
Common Name :: Hibiscus (जास्वंद)
Hibiscus is a genus of flowering plants which is quite large,comprising several hundred species that are native to warm temperate,subtropical and tropical regions throughout the world.The leaves are alternate,ovate to lanceolate,often with atoothed or lobed margin(dentate).The flowers are large,conspcuous,trumpet-shaped,with five or more petals,colour from white to pink,red,blue,orange,peach,yellow or purple,and from 4-18cm broad.
Ecological Value:Attract butterflies,bees,and hummingbirds.
Cultural Value:The red Hibiscus is the flower of God Ganesha and Goddess Kali.
Economical Value:Hibiscus flowers are used for landscaping,Bark for rope preparation,wood for making canoe floats,in beverages,as food,prescribed as medicine in Ayurveda.
