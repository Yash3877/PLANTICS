Bahava

Scientific Name : Cassia fistula L.
Family : Caesalpiniaceae
Common Name :Golden shower tree, Bahava (बहावा)
Botanical name- Vernacular name- Family- This plant is originated in Indian subcontinent and regions near South-east Asia. It is medium sized tree growing up to 33 to 60 feet in height. Stem is woody, glabrous. Leaves are unipinnately compound, large, ovate, acute. Inflorescence is drooping receme. Flowers bracteates, bright yellow in colour,with stamens 10,monocarpellary ovary. Fruit is long, cylindrical, pendulous pod with ovate seeds. Flowering in month of March to July. Fruiting is throughout the year. This plant is used as an ornamental plant. Fruit pulp is used as Ayurvedic medicines. Wood is used to make agricultural implements Bark is used for tanning.
