Putranjiva

Scientific Name : Putranjiva Roxburghii
Family : Putranjivaceae
Common Name : Putranjiva (पत्र ु ंजीवा)
Putranjiva is native to Southeast Asia, the Indian Subcontinent, Japan, Southern China, and New Guinea. Along with Drypetes, it contains mustard oils as a chemical defense against herbivores.The ability to produce glycosinolates is believed to have evolved only twice, in the Putranjivaceae and the Brassicales.
Economic Importance:This plant is used to treat many diseases such as treatment of mouth and stmouth ulcers, hot swellings, smallpox, burning sensation.Wood is used for house building,agricultural implements,tool handles and turnery.
