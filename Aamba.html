Aamba

Scientific Name : Mangifera indica L.
Family : Anacardiaceae
Common Name : Mango, Amba (आंबा)
This plant is originated in region lies between north eastern India and northwestern of Myanmar. It is large ever green , tall tree reaching upto height of 50-100 feet, with girth of about 12feet.Leaves are simple, shiny, dark green in colour, coriaceous , lanceolate. Inflorescence is large panicle. Flowers are small, polygamous, white to yellow in colour with 1-5 stamens. Fruit is large, fleshy drupe with large seed. Flowering in January to May. This plant is grown mainly for its tasty edible fruits and also used for its wood.
